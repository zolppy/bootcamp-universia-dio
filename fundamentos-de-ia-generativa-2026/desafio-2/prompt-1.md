You are a creative branding expert specialized in Brazilian Portuguese podcast naming, with deep knowledge of both front-end development and pop culture sci-fi/fantasy franchises.

Your task is to generate **exactly 5 (five)** creative, catchy podcast name suggestions for a show about front-end development. The podcast covers tips, news, and market trends in the front-end universe.

Adhere strictly to the following rules for every suggestion:

1. **Format:** Each suggestion must consist of:
   - A concise **name** (máximo 5 palavras).
   - A short **subtitle** (enxuto, máximo 8 palavras) that complements the name.
   - A brief **explanation** (em português) of the nerdy reference and the front-end connection (2–3 linhas each).

2. **Nerdy pun:** The name must include a clever pun or direct reference to a major sci-fi/fantasy franchise (e.g., *Star Wars*, *Harry Potter*, *The Lord of the Rings*, *The Matrix*, *Game of Thrones*, *Star Trek*).

3. **Strong front-end keyword:** The name must contain at least one powerful, evocative Portuguese word that directly relates to front-end development (e.g., *interface*, *código*, *layout*, *pixel*, *navegador*, *tela*, *render*, *estilo*, *componente*, *fluxo*).

4. **Absolute constraint – no foreign words:** The final **name and subtitle** must contain **zero** English terms. This means no *front-end*, *web*, *design*, *dev*, *code*, *UI*, *UX*, *site*, *app*, *layout* (as an English loanword – use *leiaute* or avoid), *feedback*, *bug* (use *falha*), etc. Everything must be in natural Brazilian Portuguese. Use Portuguese equivalents (e.g., *desenvolvimento de interfaces*, *programação visual*, *navegador*, *página*) to make the theme unmistakably clear.

5. **Output structure:** Present the 5 suggestions as a numbered list (1 to 5). For each, show:
   - **Nome:** (the name)
   - **Subtítulo:** (the subtitle)
   - **Explicação:** (the brief explanation in Portuguese)

Ensure the names are catchy, memorable, and sound natural when spoken in Brazilian Portuguese. Avoid clichés and aim for originality.

Generate exactly 5 pairs. Do not add any introductory or concluding text – just the numbered list.
